# Support DataPad++

Product information, documentation, datastore coverage, and downloads are
available from the [official DataPad++ website](https://datapad-plus-plus.org/).

DataPad++ is free to use, including for internal company and commercial work.
If the app helps you, sponsorships and donations help keep development moving.

## Everyone Who Uses DataPad++

You can use the official DataPad++ application under the DataPad++ Free Use
License 1.0. If you find it valuable, please consider sponsoring the project.

## Companies And Redistribution

Companies may use DataPad++ internally for free. A separate written license is
only needed for redistribution, bundling, white-labeling, hosted or managed
service use, publishing modified builds, or using DataPad++ source/assets to
build a competing or substitute product.

For redistribution or OEM-style licensing, contact FullMontyDevelopment
through the GitHub repository.
