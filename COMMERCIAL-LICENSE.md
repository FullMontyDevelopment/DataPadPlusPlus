# Commercial And Corporate Use

DataPad++ is free to use, including inside companies and other commercial
organizations.

The important distinction is:

- You may use the official DataPad++ application for personal, nonprofit,
  government, internal business, and commercial work.
- You may not redistribute, sell, rebrand, white-label, bundle, host, or publish
  DataPad++ or modified versions as your own product or service.

This means a company may install DataPad++, connect it to internal datastores,
run queries, create tests, inspect metrics, and use it in day-to-day work
without buying a commercial-use license.

A separate written license is required for:

- redistribution to third parties;
- bundling DataPad++ with another product, appliance, service, or consulting
  deliverable;
- offering DataPad++ or a modified version as a hosted or managed service;
- white-labeling or rebranding DataPad++;
- publishing a fork or modified build; or
- using DataPad++ source code or assets to build a competing or substitute
  product.

Sponsorships and donations are appreciated and help keep DataPad++ available,
but they are not required for ordinary internal use.
